Omelette with Radish

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken eggs
  • 300 grams of Radish
  • 1 table spoon of White soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of Seasoning Powder
  • 3 tablespoon of Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the radish thoroughly. Stir 3 eggs into the bowl.
  2. Heat the pan, add oil, add radish and stir fry until slightly brown.
  3. When the radish is yellow Put in the eggs.
  4. When the eggs are cooked, turn them over to the other side.
  5. When both sides are cooked, transfer to a plate.
  6. The omelette are now ready to be served.
